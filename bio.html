Ocelot Analysis

Ocelots are very small and agile creatures. The reason why we should study ocelots is to track how they behave, thrive in their environment, and evolve. There are many ways to conserve ocelots; the biggest factor being fragmentation. Humans alone have caused many organisms such as ocelots to be displaced from their habitats.

Distinct Characteristics & Natural History

Ocelots possess thin, sharp, teeth so the process of hunting, killing, and consuming of an organism goes smoothly. Ground color varies. Ocelots have black-bordered spots run along the shoulders and back, have 1-2 stripes on the inner leg, and the back of their ears are black with a white spot in the center.
